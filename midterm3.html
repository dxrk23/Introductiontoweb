Review :


Hatori Chise is only 16, but she has already experienced all

the hardships of life. She has no one, but her life has no

meaning. But suddenly, the gears of fate that have already

rusted, begin to move. At a difficult moment for her, the

mysterious magician offered her help, which she could not

refuse. But who is he? He looks more like a demon than a man.

Will he help her, or will he plunge into the depths of darkness?
