Review :


Hyakkao Private Academy is the place where the so-called elite

study. A prestigious educational institution for privileged

people with an unusual training program. If you are the child

of the richest of the rich, sporting achievements or education

do not matter. Only the ability to read your opponent or

knowledge of the art of making deals will help here survive.

What will hone these skills better than a rigorous gambling

curriculum? In this academy, winners live like kings, and losers

lose everything. But only with the advent of Yumeko Jabami

will students learn what it means to truly play!
